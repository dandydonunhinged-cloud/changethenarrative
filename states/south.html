TIER 1: GEORGIA HOUSE (HOT)

Current: R 99 - D 81 (180 seats). Need 91 for majority = net gain of 8-10.

Open Governor's Race: Kemp term-limited. Keisha Lance Bottoms (D) vs Burt Jones (R). Top-of-ticket energy.

Proven Targets: - HD-117 (Gwinnett): Lost by 80 votes. Mughal running again. - HD-105: Lost by 110 votes. - HD-52 (Gwinnett): Lost by ~600 votes (~2%). Kang running again. - HD-53 (Atlanta suburbs): Silcox narrowly held despite being massively outraised.

Special Election Proof: Eric Gisler flipped a Trump+12 seat (Dec 2025). Olive oil shop owner ran on healthcare + cost of living. GA Dem Party identified 17 GOP-held districts that lean further left.

Candidate Profile: Gisler model = small business owner + economic populism + ground game. NOT national culture war.

TIER 1: NC SENATE SUPERMAJORITY (1 SEAT)

Impact: Governor Stein's veto becomes unoverridable. R's already overrode 8 of 14 vetoes.

Targets: 168 of 170 districts contested by Dems. NC Educators on the Ballot running 6 teacher candidates.
